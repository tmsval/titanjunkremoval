Items We Cannot Take

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot remove certain items. Click on a card to see details.

Hazardous Chemicals

Industrial chemicals, solvents, pesticides, toxic substances.

Automotive Fluids

Motor oil, gasoline, antifreeze, brake fluids.

Propane Tanks

Grill tanks or compressed gas cylinders.

Asbestos

Materials containing asbestos require licensed removal.

Wet Paint

Paint must be fully dried before disposal.

Biohazard Waste

Medical waste, needles, and hazardous biological materials.
