Trash & Disposal Policy

Due to New Jersey waste transport regulations, Titan Junk Removal is not a traditional trash hauling company. Our services focus on removing reusable, recyclable, and donation-eligible items.

We generally do not accept bagged household garbage, loose trash, or mixed landfill waste. If you're unsure about your items, send us photos and we will let you know if we can help.

Items We Cannot Take

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot remove certain items. Click on a card to see details.
